Home/About

A house of materials.
Built across India.

We are a vertically integrated materials manufacturer operating across multiple production locations in India — from raw mineral feed through fused or sintered intermediates, fired refractory shapes, blended chemistries, and finished grades. One discipline, one chain of custody, one signature on every certificate.

— 01 / How we’re built

From mineral feed to finished grade.

Furner RefraCeram Private Limited operates as a multi-site Indian materials manufacturer. Fusion, sintering, milling, magnetic separation, pressing, firing, blending, and grading are run in-house at production locations across the country — not subcontracted, not toll-processed. The geographic spread is deliberate: it gives us redundancy on input minerals, freight flexibility on outbound shipments, and one consistent quality discipline running across every site.

Today our materials reach refractory plants, abrasive lines, steel mills, glass and cement plants, foundries, chemical processors, and tile-installation distributors across India, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Europe. We move slowly on the strategy and quickly on the order. The customers we keep are the ones who measured the bag against the spec sheet and found nothing missing.

— 02 / Discipline

Four things we don’t compromise on.

A short list, deliberately. Long lists are easy to write and hard to defend. These four are the ones we stake the company on, every order.

— 01

Material Integrity

We do not blend down. The chemistry on the COA is the chemistry in the bag, period. If a batch falls outside specification, it does not leave the gate — it goes back to processing or it goes to scrap.

— 02

Specification Discipline

Every grade has a written specification. Grit window, chemistry, density, magnetic content, packaging — all named numbers, all measured, all attached. No verbal hand-waving on the phone, ever.

— 03

Lead-Time Honesty

If the line is full, we tell you. We’d rather lose the order than promise a Tuesday and ship on a Friday. Production calendars are visible to our commercial team in real time and we plan against them, not against optimism.

— 04

After-Sale Support

A material complaint gets a senior process engineer on the phone within one business day, and a sample lot back in our lab within the week. We close the loop before the next order, not after the third one.

— 03 / At a glance

The shape of the company.

Numbers we measure ourselves by. Capability snapshots, not marketing — a customer briefing in five lines.

— 01

Vertical from raw to grade

Fusion, milling, sizing, magnetic separation, and packing — all in-house. No tolling, no outsourced grading.

— 02

Five product categories

Abrasive grains, refractory grains, refractory products, industrial chemicals, and construction chemicals — thirty-plus distinct product lines under one COA system.

— 03

International standards

FEPA, JIS, and P-series for abrasives. ASTM, IS, DIN for refractories. IS 15477 and EN 12004 for construction chemicals. USP and BP grades available for industrial chemicals on request.

— 04

Export-capable from day one

PP bags, jumbo bags, drums, kits, and palletised brick and shape consignments. Sea, container, or break-bulk — built for export to any port we can reach.
