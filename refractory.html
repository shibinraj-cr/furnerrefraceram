Refractory Grains.
For the heat that won’t quit.

High-purity aggregates engineered to survive 1700 °C and corrosive slag. Sintered tabular alumina, fused alumina, and silicon carbide — graded for monolithics, bricks, and castables.

— 01 / Refractory Grains

Tabular Alumina

Sintered α-Al₂O₃ aggregate with characteristic tabular crystal habit. Very low closed porosity, high density, and exceptional thermal-shock resistance — the gold standard for high-end refractory castables.

Castables Monolithics Steel Ladles Petrochem

Chemistry

Al₂O₃ ≥ 99.2%  ·  Na₂O ≤ 0.4%  ·  Fe₂O₃ ≤ 0.05%

Physical

  • Bulk Density≥ 3.55 g/cm³
  • Apparent Porosity≤ 5%
  • Refractoriness≥ 1790 °C

Aggregate Size

  • Coarse3–6 mm
  • Medium1–3 mm
  • Fine0–1 mm
  • Powder200 / 325 mesh

Packing

  • PP Bag25 kg
  • Jumbo1000 kg
— 02 / Refractory Grains

White Fused Alumina (Refractory Grade)

Refractory-grade WFA fused from low-soda Bayer alumina. High purity, low thermal expansion, excellent corrosion resistance against acid slags and molten glass.

Castables Glass Furnaces Investment Casting

Chemistry

Al₂O₃ ≥ 99.0%  ·  Na₂O ≤ 0.5%

Aggregate Size

  • Coarse3–5 mm
  • Medium1–3 mm
  • Fine0–1 mm
  • Powder200 mesh

Packing

  • PP Bag25 kg
  • Jumbo1000 kg
— 03 / Refractory Grains

Brown Fused Alumina (Refractory Grade)

Tough, blocky brown fused alumina aggregate engineered for high mechanical loading at temperature. Standard in steel-side castables, kiln furniture, and shock-resistant monolithic linings.

Steel Ladles Kiln Furniture Castables

Chemistry

Al₂O₃ ≥ 95%  ·  SiO₂ ≤ 1.5%  ·  TiO₂ ≤ 3.5%

Aggregate Size

  • Coarse3–6 mm
  • Medium1–3 mm
  • Fine0–1 mm
  • Powder200 / 325 mesh

Packing

  • PP Bag25 kg
  • Jumbo1000 kg
— 04 / Refractory Grains

Black Silicon Carbide (Refractory Grade)

Refractory-grade SiC for kiln furniture, ladle linings, blast-furnace troughs, and other extreme-thermal-shock environments. High thermal conductivity, low thermal expansion, alkali-resistant.

Kiln Furniture Foundry Blast Furnace Trough Linings

Chemistry

  • Jumbo1000 kg
