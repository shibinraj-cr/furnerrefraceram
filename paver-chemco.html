— About the Division

A focused brand for India’s toughest climate.

Paver Chemco is Furner RefraCeram’s construction-chemicals division, dedicated to a single problem — chemistry that holds up in 3,000+ mm of annual rainfall, year-round humidity, and 40 °C+ summer heat. Every product is formulated, tested, and proven for the tropical monsoon belt.

From AAC block walls to vitrified-tile facades to terrace waterproofing, Paver Chemco serves builders who refuse to gamble with the monsoon.

— 02 / Built for Tropical Climates

Engineered for the conditions India actually has.

South India’s monsoon belt is not a niche — it is the brief. Paver Chemco products are formulated against the climate they will actually live in.

— 01

Monsoon Ready

Tested for 3,000+ mm annual rainfall and year-round high-humidity conditions characteristic of the South Indian monsoon belt.

Heat Tolerant

Engineered to perform in 40 °C+ summer temperatures without bond failure, polymer breakdown, or thermal-movement cracking.

AAC Block Compatible
