— 03 / How an engagement works

Four steps. No mystery.

Engagements range from a single half-day call up to a multi-week deep dive. The shape is the same; only the depth varies.

— 01

Brief

A 30-minute call. You describe the line, the application, the symptom or the decision in front of you. We confirm whether we can help — and what kind of engagement fits.

— 02

Diagnose

Sample analysis in our lab, supplier-COA review, optional site visit. We look at the same numbers your team is looking at, plus the ones you didn’t know to ask for.

— 03

Recommend

A written report — specification options, supplier shortlist, QC protocol, or root-cause findings. In plain English. With the numbers attached.

— 04

Support

An optional post-engagement window — typically four to twelve weeks — for follow-up questions as you implement. No new clock starts until you ask for it.

— 05 / What you walk away with

Documents you can actually use.

Every engagement closes with deliverables that survive past the engagement — written, attached to numbers, ready to drop into your QC system or your tender pack. Nothing verbal-only.

— 01

Specification document

A finalised spec sheet covering chemistry, grit window, physical properties, and acceptance criteria — drop-in replacement for whatever document your supplier ships against today.

— 02

Sample & COA protocol

A repeatable sampling plan and COA template tied to your acceptance criteria, so the next batch is checked against the same numbers as the last one.

— 03

Supplier shortlist (optional)

A vetted shortlist with audit notes and sample-lot data — not a directory, a working list. Includes us only if we’re actually the right fit.

— 04

Findings report

For diagnostic engagements: root-cause analysis with the supporting data, plus prioritised remediation options ranked by cost and time-to-implement.
