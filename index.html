— 01 / The House

A materials house, not a trading post.

We fuse, sinter, mill, press, fire, and formulate in-house. From raw mineral feed through finished grain, fired shape, blended chemistry, and packed bag — every step lives under one quality discipline. The result is repeatability — batch to batch, drawing to drawing, year to year.

Our materials reach abrasive lines, refractory plants, steel mills, glass tanks, cement kilns, foundries, chemical processors, and tile-installation contractors across India, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Europe. They keep coming back because our specification sheet is what arrives in the bag, drum, or pallet.
